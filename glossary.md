# Glossary

+ **URI (Uniform Resource Identifier)**: A sequence of characters used to identify a specific, logical, or physical resource on the internet or a private network. Examples:
    `https://www.google.com` All URLs are URIs,
    `urn:isbn:0973402974` All URNs are URIs,
    `mailto:john.doe@example.com`
+ **URL (Uniform Resource Locator)**: A type of URI that identifies a resource by its location and access method. Example: `https://www.google.com`.
+ **URN (Uniform Resource Name)**: A type of URI that identifies a resource by a persistent name, regardless of its location or accessibility. Example: `urn:isbn:0973402974`.
